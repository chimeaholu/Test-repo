## Dependency Check

### `B-047` Telemetry Ingestion API Profile

- Required upstreams present:
  - `B-046` sensor event schema and provenance contract at `67c50c66`
  - `B-035` tool contract registry at `551b4b71`

  - idempotency and resume tokens are tracked at the API seam, which is the correct layer for transport replay semantics
  - schema validation is enforced through the existing tool-contract registry rather than ad hoc batch parsing

### `B-020` Finance Partner Decision Adapter

- Required upstreams present:
  - `B-011` wallet ledger service at `38ef659e`
  - `B-001` country pack resolver at `1a17d74a`
- Integration posture is coherent:
  - partner decisions validate country/currency policy through the shared country pack instead of re-encoding market rules
  - the adapter stays outside ledger mutation paths and only defines the regulated decision boundary
  - idempotent request replay is explicit, which is required before later finance HITL and payout flows

### `B-021` Insurance Parametric Trigger Registry

- Required upstreams present:
  - `B-017` climate risk ingestion at `ac5bba1b`
  - `B-020` finance partner decision adapter at `d1624a9e`
- Integration posture is coherent:
  - payout events only emerge from approved insurance decisions, which prevents climate signals from bypassing the partner boundary
  - threshold evaluation consumes normalized climate metrics rather than duplicating raw ingest logic
  - event emission is deterministic and deduped on trigger/decision/signal identity
